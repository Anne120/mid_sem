Description

One of the tastier joints of lamb, is a slower roasting joint full of depth. Our rolled and boned slamb makes for easy carving. When cooked, the naturally occurring marbles of fat melt away making the meat deliciously juicy and tender. Our top tip is to score the meat, cook slowly with rosemary, garlic and a good glug of wine.
